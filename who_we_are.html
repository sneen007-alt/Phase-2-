Our Heritage

A Legacy of
Persuasion.

Founded in 1994, the UMD Sales Club began as a small group of business students looking to improve their pitch skills. Over three decades, it has evolved into a cornerstone of the Labovitz School.

Today, we are one of the most decorated student organizations in the University of Minnesota system, consistently placing in the top 10 at the National Collegiate Sales Competition.
